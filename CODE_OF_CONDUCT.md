## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to creating a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and kind
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Helping other contributors learn and grow
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others‚Äô private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action against unacceptable behavior
- Reviewing and moderating contributions fairly
- Ensuring a safe environment for everyone

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and chats
- Community events
- Any official project communication spaces

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Appropriate corrective action will be taken if necessary.

---

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent removal from the project

---

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help maintain a respectful and inclusive community.
